# Final Positioning and Copy

## Positioning

**Category:** Forensic Evidence Intelligence for Family Law

**Five-second takeaway:** TruthTrace turns scattered family-law evidence into a structured, source-linked record that parents and professionals can review.

The homepage does not lead with AI and does not position TruthTrace as a chatbot, custody predictor, diagnostic engine, legal adviser, surveillance platform, generic organizer, or court decision-maker.

## Navigation

- Platform → `#platform`
- Parents → `#parents`
- Professionals → `#professionals`
- Trust → `#trust`
- Technology remains available in mobile navigation and the footer.

There is no right-side conversion or sign-in action because no approved functioning destination exists.

## Hero

**Eyebrow**

> FORENSIC EVIDENCE INTELLIGENCE FOR FAMILY LAW

**Headline**

> Turn scattered evidence into a record you can follow.

**Supporting copy**

> TruthTrace organizes supported messages, documents, images, and exports into a structured chronology with citations back to exact source locations—so parents and professionals can review the record without losing the evidence trail.

**Boundary**

> Built to support evidence review—not replace legal or professional judgment.

**Primary action:** Explore the platform → `#command-center`

**Secondary action:** Inspect the proof chain → `#proof-chain`

**Disclosure**

> Illustrative product demonstration · Illustrative case data · No real family information shown · No evidence uploads on this site

**Trust line**

> Source identity visible in this demonstration. Citations inspectable. Human review required.

The trust line intentionally replaces the unproven preferred-draft phrase “Sources preserved in validated workflows.”

## Six-section sequence

1. **Platform / hero and command center** — exact value, boundary, disclosure, and coordinated product proof.
2. **From fragments to structure** — evidence types and Register → Structure → Connect → Review → Prepare sequence.
3. **Proof difference** — Observation → Timeline event → Citation → Exact excerpt → Source record, with interpretation boundary.
4. **Audience pathways** — separate parent and legal-professional responsibilities.
5. **Trust architecture** — Source record → Evidence identity → Structured event → Citation → Human review → Reviewable output.
6. **Closing action** — “Review the record, not a black box.” with a real proof-chain link.

The footer is outside the six-section count.

## Browse-only conversion decision

No approved pilot request, contact intake, login, dashboard, waitlist, scheduler, payment, upload, or professional-briefing workflow exists in the repository or public route set. The final CTA strategy is therefore honest internal inspection. The contact page remains a do-not-submit boundary and does not invent an intake channel.
